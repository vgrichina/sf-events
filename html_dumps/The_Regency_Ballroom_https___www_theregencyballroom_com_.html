Built in 1909, The Regency Center is considered the finest example of Scottish Rite temple architecture in the country, featuring Gothic, Neoclassic, and Beaux-Arts styles. This versatile San Francisco landmark offers three separate venues - The Regency Ballroom, Social Hall SF and The Lodge - each with unique architectural characteristics and an array of accompanying rooms.
